About Kolhua

Kolhua is a small Village/hamlet in Brahmpur Block in Gorakhpur District of Uttar Pradesh State, India. It comes under Kolhua Panchayath. It belongs to Gorakhpur Division . It is located 31 KM towards South from District head quarters Gorakhpur. 302 KM from State capital Lucknow
Kolhua Pin code is 273202 and postal head office is Sardar Nagar .
Kolhua is surrounded by Kauri Ram Block towards west , Sardarnagar Block towards North , Gauri Bazar Block towards East , Khorabar Block towards North .
Rudrapur , Deoria , Gorakhpur , Sahjanwa are the near by Cities to Kolhua.
This Place is in the border of the Gorakhpur District and Deoria District. Deoria District Gauri Bazar is East towards this place.
